<div id="eslefl" style="margin: .5em 0; text-align: left; clear: both;"><strong>ESL or EFL?</strong> What do ESL and EFL mean? ESL stands for English as a Second Language. EFL stands for English as a Foreign Language. ESL is used when people learn English in an English speaking country. EFL is used when people learn English in a non-English speaking country. Is this important? Not very, if you are a learner. But there are some important technical considerations for teachers.</div>
